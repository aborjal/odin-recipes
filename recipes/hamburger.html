Hamburger

Home

Description

This is a simple recipe, very easy to follow, a lot of the ingredients you could find them in your kitchen.

It is enjoyed by a lot of people, perfect for every meal.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bread
  • 250 grams of meat
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Bacon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your frying pan.
  2. Fry the bacon and leave it on the side, keep the pan on the stove.
  3. Then add a little bit of oil
  4. Separate your meat into four balls and add salt and pepper.
  5. Add the meat to the pan and make them into a patty with the help of an spatula.
  6. Once it is almost cooked, add the cheese on top.
  7. Once the cheese is melted take out the meat and put it on the side.
  8. To prepare the burger, put on the burger bread the meat, the bacon, the lettuce and the tomatoes, add any sauce of your preference.
