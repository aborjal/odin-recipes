Description

This is a recipe, easy to make with just a few ingredients

It is also very quickly to prepare

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Spaghetti
  • Basil
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and cut the garlic into small pieces.
  2. On a heated cooking pot add some oil.
  3. Once the oil is a bit hot, add the onion and the garlic, and cook them for 7 minutes, also add a bit of salt and pepper.
  4. While the onion and garlic are cooking, cut the tomatoes into pieces, not too small.
  5. Add the tomatoes to the cooking pot and move them, let them cook for 10 minutes.
  6. On another pot add some water and boil it.
  7. Add the basil leaves to the tomatoes and stir it well.
  8. Taste it and if it needs more salt or pepper, add them.
  9. On the boiled water add some salt and put the spaghetti for 10 minutes and the take out the spaghetti.
  10. Turn off the pot of the tomatoes and mix it with the spaghetti.
