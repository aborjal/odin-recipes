Description

This recipe is very easy to follow, with just a few ingredients you can make this very delicious pizza.

This recipe has been used hundreds of times, and people have enjoy it, so if you end up being part of those people, make sure to share the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of Pizza sauce
  • 200 grams Mozarella cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. On your clean table start preparing the dough, stretch it until you have it on a circular shape.
  3. Put the pizza sauce on the dough and spread it evenly.
  4. Put the cheese and the basil on top of the sauce.
  5. Put a little of salt and pepper on top of your pizza.
  6. Put the pizza in the oven and after 30 minutes take it out and enjoy it.
